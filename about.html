About Us

SBafashi Digital Solutions is a leading provider of digital solutions based in Kano, Nigeria. Located at the Mechatronics Department of Aliko Dangote University of Science and Technology, Wudil, we specialize in a range of digital services including website design, application development, software development, AI training, and software consultancy.

Our mission is to empower businesses and individuals with innovative technology solutions that drive growth and efficiency. With a team of experienced professionals, we are committed to delivering quality services and exceeding client expectations.

We also provide sales and servicing of computers and printers, ensuring our clients have access to the best digital tools and maintenance services.
